## Why this blog exists

I write to understand what I learned. Putting it into words forces me to close the gaps I skip past while coding — and what's left stays on the record for whoever comes next (including me, six months from now).

The goal is simple: build, in public and with consistency, an honest **technical reference**. No pretending I know more than I do, no shortcut to authority. The tone is the straightforward one I appreciate on [TabNews](https://www.tabnews.com.br) and the depth I follow on blogs like [Fidelissauro's](https://fidelissauro.dev).

## What you'll find here

- **Learnings** — what I figured out solving a real problem, with the context I wish I'd had.
- **Opinions** — takes on engineering and career, stated with the why.
- **Digests** — condensed notes from what I study, to revisit without rereading everything.
- **[Reading]({{< relref "/reading" >}})** — articles and books that shape how I think about engineering and career.

## Who writes

I'm **Gustavo Carvalho**, a software engineer. I build technical presence through consistency, not shortcuts — one post at a time.

Reach out or dig through the code on [GitHub](https://github.com/carvalhosauro).
